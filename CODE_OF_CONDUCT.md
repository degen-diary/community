# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in the Degen Diary community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

**Positive behavior includes:**

- Being respectful and constructive in feedback
- Welcoming different viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**

- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Harassment in any form, public or private
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Spam, scams, or unsolicited promotions
- Sharing financial advice (this is a journaling tool, not financial advice)

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all Degen Diary community spaces, including this GitHub repository, our Discord server, and any other official channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **hello@degen-diary.xyz**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. The team reserves the right to remove comments, issues, or ban users who violate this Code of Conduct.
